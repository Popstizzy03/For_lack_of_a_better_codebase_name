About Deaujo Jewellery

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Deaujo Jewellery has been crafting exquisite pieces that blend traditional artisanship with modern design. Our journey began in a small workshop in Lusaka, Zambia, fueled by a passion for creating timeless beauty.

Our Commitment

At Deaujo, we are committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices. Each piece is crafted with care, using responsibly sourced materials and supporting local artisans.

Our Promise

We promise to deliver not just jewelry, but wearable art that tells a story. Every Deaujo piece is a testament to quality, creativity, and the enduring allure of fine craftsmanship.
